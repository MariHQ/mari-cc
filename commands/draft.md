Run the **draft** verb from the Mari skill: `$ARGUMENTS` is the brief. Follow `references/reference-draft.md`: load `PRODUCT.md` and `STYLE.md`, read representative project writing, outline, write in the team's register and voice, self-edit, then verify with `mari detect` before presenting. If the document matches a known archetype (RFC, ADR, runbook, or postmortem), scaffold with `mari asset scaffold <type>` and honor its required sections.
